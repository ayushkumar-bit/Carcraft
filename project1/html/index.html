Rooms & Suites

One King Bed Suite

The King Bed Suite combines both practicality and distinct areas, featuring a roomy bedroom with a king-size bed and a separate living space furnished with a sofa bed. This suite is suitable for accommodating up to four individuals and comes with premium amenities to enhance the experience for the whole family.

The luxury Penthouse

The Penthouse Suite is a stunning option for a special occasion or multiple night stay with friends or family, with large spacious Balcony, perfect for entertaining or simply relaxing and enjoying the peaceful and tranquil surroundings.

Queen Standard Room

This room offers simplicity and comfort, featuring a Queen-sized bed and suitable for up to two guests. It’s an ideal choice for business travelers, thanks to its stylish desk, and it’s also convenient for families, as it comes equipped with a mini fridge, microwave, and coffee maker, allowing you to store and reheat leftovers from local restaurants. This standard room is a fantastic option for those seeking affordable travel without compromising essential amenities.
